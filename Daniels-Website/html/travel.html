Australia

I had the pleasure of jumping and living in Australia for 1.5 years. Here I got to experience skyidiving over beautiful landscapes and beaches I also worked as a parachute packer where I would pack parachutes for people doing their first jump.

I packed over 3000 parachutes here and sometimes up to 80 a day. This was hard work but I managed to save a lot of money which has been one of the main factors allowing me to return to education.

Russia

Here I got to experience skydiving from Soviet planes and helicopters such as the MIL-8 (pictured right) and the Let L-410. It was surreal skydiving somewhere with such a different culture to ourselves. It was customary when doing something new to buy a lot of ice cream and give it out to everyone which was cool to experience

I also trained in the tunnel in Moscow for 10 hours overall. Here I learnt a lot of freeflying from local instructors.

Algarve, Portugal

Algarve is an amazing area to go for a skydiving holiday. They have beautiful weather year round and a lovely facilities. Since the area is a well known tourist destination, the restaurants and accomodation available are amazing. I visited in winter where the area wasn't too busy, so I got to enjoy Algarve without the crowds.

This dropzone is very international and has instructors and coaches from around the world. Making it a great place for people to learn to skydive in good weather.
