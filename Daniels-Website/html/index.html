My Experience

I have been skydiving since 2012 and in this time, completed around 600 jumps. This adventure has brought me to 5 different countries where I have jumped, trained and worked. I worked as a parachute packer at a busy dropzone in Australia. I also visited Russia where I trained in a wind tunnel.

My local dropzone is in county Ofally where I have done the majority of my jumps. The atmosphere there is great and they have the best facilities in Ireland.

One of the limiting factors of skydiving in Ireland is weather. To jump we need winds of lower than usually 20mph and no rain/thick clouds. Despite this Ireland is an amazing place to skydive.
